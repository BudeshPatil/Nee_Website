Founder and Design Director of Neelgund, bringing over 15 years of distinguished experience in interior architecture, crafting thoughtfully designed spaces that seamlessly blend functionality with aesthetic excellence.

Our design philosophy

Neelgund blends contemporary aesthetics with timeless elegance. We approach each project with precision, artistry and a deep understanding of unique requirements of our clients. From conceptualisation to final styling, our mission is to craft refined, functional and enduring spaces with practical design solutions.

Our Process

Driven by Purpose, Designed with Precision

Integrity in Every Detail.

PROJECT INITIATION

Every project begins with a comprehensive discussion with the operator and owner to understand their vision, identity, DNA and preferences.

RESEARCH AND IDEATION

We create a distinct sense of place by understanding the culture, history and character of its surroundings and inhabitants.

CREATIVE SPACE PLANNING

Different zones and their relationships are developed through the creative process, considering structure, size and orientation to maximise comfort and interaction.

DEVELOPING A FEEL AND MOOD

The building and its surroundings shape our interior design approach. Architecture plays a major part in determining our design, particularly the volume of space.

SKETCHING

Pen to paper enables us to visualise and crystallise desired outcomes. We proceed with multiple refinements until no detail is overlooked.

3D RENDERINGS

Visual interpretations help clients understand the design clearly. Collectively, the exchange of feedback smoothens the design phases and flow.
