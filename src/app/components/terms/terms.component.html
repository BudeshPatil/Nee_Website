Terms of Use for Construction & Development Projects

These terms govern the use of our website, project listings, partner tools, and communications for construction and land development developers.

1. Eligibility and Access

Use of our platform is available to individuals and businesses who agree to comply with all terms. If you are using the site on behalf of a company or project team, you represent that you are authorized to act for that organization.

2. Account Responsibilities

Construction and development developers, along with project stakeholders, are responsible for keeping account credentials secure. Any activity conducted under your account is your responsibility. Notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

3. Project Content and Listings

All project descriptions, images, and technical details are provided for informational purposes. While we aim for accuracy, you should verify site plans, pricing, and delivery timelines directly with the construction project owner or our development partner.

4. Acceptable Use

You may not misuse the site, submit false project data, harass other users, or circumvent our systems. Broadcast or automated scraping of project listings is prohibited.

5. Intellectual Property

The website design, content, and brand assets are protected by copyright and trademark law. You may not reproduce or redistribute images, text, or project details without permission.

Privacy Policy for Construction & Development Developers

We respect your privacy and explain how we collect, use, and protect personal information from site visitors, construction developers, and potential buyers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect contact details, project inquiries, browsing behavior, and communication data. This includes data submitted through forms, service requests, calls, and messages.

2. How We Use Your Data

Personal information is used to respond to inquiries, manage project requests, improve our platform, and send relevant updates about our development services.

3. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, so please share sensitive information only through trusted channels.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for hosting, analytics, messaging, and payment support. These providers receive only the information needed to perform their services.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us through the support channel if you need to update your details.
